HOW TO LAUNCH CALAMITY WAR V0.10

LOCAL TEST:
1. Unzip calamity_war_demo_v0_8.zip.
2. Open the unzipped folder.
3. Double-click index.html.

GITHUB PAGES:
1. Open the unzipped folder.
2. Upload the folder CONTENTS to the repository root.
3. Make sure index.html is beside assets/.
4. Commit changes.
5. Wait for GitHub Pages to refresh.

NEW IN V0.10:
- Akila corrected from low-stat sleepy entrant to hidden god-tier misdirection.
- Akila now has stronger gameplay stats, hidden-power bio text, Eyes Open special, and battle aura.

- PvP and Training now support 1–3 fighter teams.
- PvP supports 1v1, 1v2, 2v3, 3v3, and any team-size combination.
- Training supports a 1–3 fighter practice team.
- Random fighter/team and random stage are included.
