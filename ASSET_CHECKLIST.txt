Calamity War V0.5 JPEG-only asset checklist

These files must be in the assets folder next to index.html:
./assets/main_menu.jpg
./assets/character_select.jpg
./assets/bg_dojo_ruins.jpg
./assets/bg_forest.jpg
./assets/bg_fortress_courtyard.jpg
./assets/bg_fortress_hall.jpg

If every image is invalid, usually the assets folder is missing, renamed, nested one level too deep, or index.html is being opened inside the ZIP instead of after extracting.
